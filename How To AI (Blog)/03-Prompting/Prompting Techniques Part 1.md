## Core Techniques
1. **Interview prompting** — "Interview me. Ask ONE question at a time." Let AI gather context before producing output
2. **AskUserQuestion pattern** — force Claude to generate a clickable form for clarification
3. **Context files over clever prompts** — .md files in your Cowork folder
4. **Extended Thinking** — always enable; forces the model to reason before answering
5. **Push back** — "This sounds like AI. Rewrite [part]." Iterate, don't accept first drafts.
