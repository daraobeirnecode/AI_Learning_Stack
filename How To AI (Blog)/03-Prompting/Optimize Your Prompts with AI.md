## The Meta-Prompt Approach
Use AI to improve your own prompts:
1. Write your prompt draft
2. Ask: "Critique this prompt. What's missing? What's vague?"
3. Iterate until the prompt is specific and constrained

## The Taste Interview for Prompts
Just as you can capture your writing voice in an .md file, you can capture your prompting patterns. Create a `prompt-style.md` with your preferences for structure, length, tone, and constraints.
