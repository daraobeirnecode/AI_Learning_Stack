## Advanced Techniques
1. **Chain-of-thought** — "Show every reasoning step, no matter how small"
2. **Socratic method** — state thesis, ask AI to destroy it, defend/revise
3. **Devil's advocate** — "List the 3 strongest objections, with evidence"
4. **Rubber duck debugging** — narrate the problem to AI as a silent listener
5. **Self-testing** — have AI quiz you on content you just created together

## Anti-Dumb Prompts
From "How ChatGPT Makes You Dumb":
- Write first, then bring in AI (not the reverse)
- Prompt for the answer AND the reasoning
- Cross-examine: "How did you get from Step 4 to Step 5? Cite data."
- Keep only steps you can restate in your own words
