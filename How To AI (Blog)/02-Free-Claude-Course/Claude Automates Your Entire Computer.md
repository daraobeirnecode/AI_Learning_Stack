## What It Is
Claude can now click, type, and navigate your computer for you — while you're away. You can text Claude from your phone to check in or add instructions mid-task.

## Setup Steps

### 1. Access Requirements
- Mac only (Windows coming soon)
- Desktop app: [claude.ai/download](https://claude.ai/download)
- Personal plan (Pro $20 or Max $100)

### 2. Enable Computer Use
Settings (bottom left) → Desktop app → General:
- Turn on **Browser use**
- Turn on **Computer use** → accept by clicking "Turn on"
- Grant **Accessibility** and **Screen recording** permissions
- **Deny apps** you want protected (e.g., 1Password)

### 3. Connect Claude Dispatch (Phone Control)
1. Claude desktop app → left sidebar → **Dispatch**
2. Click **Get started**
3. Scan QR code with your phone (need Claude app on phone, same account)
4. Turn on all desired settings
5. Click **Finish setup**

## How It Works
You text Claude from your phone: "Post this on LinkedIn," "Book a restaurant for Friday," "Research competitors and put findings in a spreadsheet."

Claude opens apps, clicks buttons, fills forms, navigates websites — all on your Mac while you're away. You get updates on your phone.

## Key Settings
- Now available for Teams plans too
- Can deny specific apps from Claude's access
- Claude takes screenshots to understand what's on screen
