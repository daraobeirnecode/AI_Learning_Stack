## Why People Are Switching
Creators, teams, and founders have quietly moved from ChatGPT to Claude. The key reasons:
- Claude writes more naturally — less "AI-sounding" output
- Cowork operates on your actual files (not just chat)
- Opus 4.6 leads coding benchmarks and reasoning
- The .md file system (context > prompts) produces dramatically better results

## What to Unlearn from ChatGPT
1. **Stop writing long, clever prompts** — with Claude, the game is text files
2. **Stop copy-pasting** — Claude reads your actual tools via Connectors
3. **Stop using default settings** — always select Opus 4.6 + Extended Thinking

## What to Learn for Claude
1. Create .md files about yourself, your style, your rules
2. Build a Cowork folder structure (see [[Cowork Setup]])
3. Use `AskUserQuestion` pattern — let Claude interview you
4. Think of Claude as an employee who needs SOPs, not a chatbot that needs prompts
