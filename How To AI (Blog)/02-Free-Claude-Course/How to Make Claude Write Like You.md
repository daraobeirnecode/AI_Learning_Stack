## The Big Realization
Your entire "voice" — the thing you think is special — is just a collection of patterns you've never written down. The moment you articulate it, it becomes portable, transferable, reproducible.

**Taste isn't what you like — it's what you reject.**

80% of Ruben's voice file is what he's NOT:
- Does NOT start with "In today's fast-paced world..."
- Does NOT use "utilize," "leverage," or "synergy"
- Does NOT write paragraphs longer than 3 sentences
- Does NOT hedge with "I think" or "perhaps" or "it seems"
- Does NOT end with summaries of what he just said

## The Process: The 100-Question Taste Interview

### Setup
1. Open Claude desktop app → Cowork tab
2. Select Opus 4.6 + Extended Thinking
3. Paste the interview prompt below

### The Interview Prompt
```
You are a Taste Interviewer — a relentless interviewer whose job 
is to extract the DNA of how I think, write, and see the world.

Conduct 100 questions across these categories:

BELIEFS & CONTRARIAN TAKES (15 questions)
- What I believe that others don't
- Hot takes I'd defend to the death

WRITING MECHANICS (20 questions)
- How I actually write (not how I think I write)
- Default sentence structures, openings, closings
- Words I overuse / love / would never use

AESTHETIC CRIMES (15 questions)
- What makes me cringe in others' writing
- Phrases that feel like nails on a chalkboard

VOICE & PERSONALITY (15 questions)
- How I use humor
- Tone when serious vs casual

STRUCTURAL PREFERENCES (15 questions)
- How I organize ideas
- Relationship with lists, headers, bullets

HARD NOs (10 questions)
- Things I'd never write about
- Approaches I'd never take

RED FLAGS (10 questions)
- What makes me immediately distrust content

RULES:
1. ONE question at a time
2. Push back on vague answers
3. Ask for specific examples
4. Call out contradictions
5. Go deeper on interesting threads
6. Don't accept "I don't know" easily
```

### Output
After 100 questions, Claude compiles a comprehensive VOICE PROFILE markdown document. Save this as your `about-me.md` and drop it in your Cowork folder.

## Key Insight
When you prompt AI without context, it defaults to the statistical middle. It doesn't lack taste — it lacks *your* taste, because you never truly gave it.

"Give AI context" is advice everyone's heard. But most people say useless things like "write conversationally." You need to say: "I write like I'm explaining something to a smart friend who's slightly impatient and will stop reading if I waste their time."
