## Claude Dispatch
Connect your phone to Claude's desktop app via **Dispatch**:
1. Claude desktop app → left sidebar → **Dispatch**
2. Click **Get started** → scan QR code with phone
3. Need Claude app on phone (same account)

## How It Works
Text Claude from your phone while it works on your Mac. Examples:
- "Post this on LinkedIn"
- "Hey, how is it going? Make sure to not forget [this]"
- "Research competitors and put findings in a spreadsheet"

Claude opens apps, clicks buttons, fills forms on your computer — you get updates on your phone.

## Requirements
- Mac only (Windows coming)
- Personal paid account (Pro or Max)
- Desktop app + phone app on same account
