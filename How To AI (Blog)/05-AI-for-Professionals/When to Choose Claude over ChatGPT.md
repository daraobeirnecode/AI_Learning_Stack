# When to Choose Claude over ChatGPT

> **Source:** Ruben Hassid, *How to AI*
> **Section:** [[How to AI - Index#📂 AI for Professionals, Leaders & Executives]]

---

## Choose Claude When
- Writing that needs to sound human (not AI-sounding)
- Working with your actual files (Cowork > ChatGPT's web agent)
- Coding projects (Claude Code dominates enterprise)
- Long-context reasoning (1M+ tokens)
- Team adoption (Projects, Skills, Plugins)

## Choose ChatGPT When
- Need image generation (DALL-E)
- Need video generation (Sora)
- Web-based automation (Agent mode)
- Custom GPTs for specific workflows
- Real-time search (though Grok is better)
