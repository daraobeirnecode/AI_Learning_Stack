> **Source:** Ruben Hassid, *How to AI*
> **Section:** [[How to AI - Index#📂 AI for Professionals, Leaders & Executives]]

## Concept
Use Claude with rich context files to create a personal mentor that knows your industry, goals, and constraints. The .md file system + Cowork makes this possible for $20/month.

## Setup
1. Create detailed context files about your business, goals, challenges
2. Upload industry reports, frameworks, case studies to your Cowork folder
3. Use the interview prompt to have Claude deeply understand your situation
4. Ask strategic questions — Claude responds with your full context
