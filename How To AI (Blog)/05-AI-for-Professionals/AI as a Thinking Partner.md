# AI as a Thinking Partner

> **Source:** Ruben Hassid, *How to AI*
> **Section:** [[How to AI - Index#📂 AI for Professionals, Leaders & Executives]]

---

## Key Concept
AI is most powerful when used as a sparring partner, not a content generator. The best use: force AI to challenge your thinking.

## Techniques
- **Socratic prompting** — state your thesis, ask AI to attack it
- **Devil's advocate** — "Give me the 3 strongest counter-arguments"
- **Rubber duck debugging** — narrate the problem aloud to AI
- **Write first, then AI** — don't start with AI; bring it in after your first draft

## Anti-Dumb Framework
From "How ChatGPT Makes You Dumb": BCG consultants using GPT-4 were 25% faster and 40% higher quality. But 83% of ChatGPT users failed to recall their own sentences. Use AI to enhance thinking, not replace it.
