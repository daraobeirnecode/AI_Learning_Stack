## Claude in Excel
Install: Excel → Insert → Get Add-ins → search "Claude by Anthropic" → Add. Requires paid Claude plan.

### First Prompts
- "Give me a summary of each tab"
- "Explain what the formula in cell B12 does in plain English"
- "Find all #REF and #VALUE errors"
- "Create a pivot summary of monthly revenue by product category"

### Create Excel from Scratch (via Cowork)
```
Create a professional Excel spreadsheet (.xlsx) for: [PURPOSE]
Context: [WHO/HOW USED]
Cover: [WHAT TO TRACK]
Rules:
- Use Excel formulas, never hardcoded
- Editable assumptions in labeled cells
- Freeze top row, auto-fit columns
```
