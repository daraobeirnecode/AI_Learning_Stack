## What It Is
Open-source workflow automation with AI reasoning layer. Self-hosted, free, connects to any API.

## AI Automation vs Traditional
- Traditional: "When X happens, do Y" (rigid)
- AI automation: Add reasoning layer — AI classifies, decides, drafts, routes

## Example Workflows
- Email arrives → AI summarizes, classifies, drafts response, routes to team
- Blog published → AI generates LinkedIn post, Twitter thread, newsletter excerpt
- Form submitted → AI scores lead, sends personalized email
