## 🔑 Key Themes Across All Posts
- **Context > Prompts**: Text files (.md) with your identity, style, and rules beat clever prompts
- **Cowork is the star**: Claude Cowork is Ruben's #1 recommended tool for knowledge workers
- **Always use Opus 4.6 + Extended Thinking**
- **The "taste interview"**: 100-question process to capture your voice in an .md file
- **AskUserQuestion pattern**: Force Claude to ask you questions before executing
- **Folder structure matters**: ABOUT ME / TEMPLATES / PROJECTS / CLAUDE OUTPUTS
