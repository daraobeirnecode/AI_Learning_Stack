> **Source:** Ruben Hassid, *How to AI*
> **Section:** [[How to AI - Index#📂 ChatGPT]]

---

## What It Is
ChatGPT's web-based agent that uses a virtual browser to navigate websites, fill forms, click buttons, and take actions on your behalf.

## How to Access
Click the + dropdown → select Agent mode.

## Best For
- Web-based tasks (data scraping, research, travel booking)
- Form filling and online submissions
- Multi-site research

## vs Claude Cowork
- ChatGPT Agent = web-based (browser)
- Claude Cowork = local files (your computer's filesystem)
