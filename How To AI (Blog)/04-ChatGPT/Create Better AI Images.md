## Tool Ranking for Images
1. **NanoBanana Pro** — clean, believable, editable; best for professional work
2. **Gemini + Imagen 4** — best overall image model
3. **Midjourney** — best for artistic/stunning images (can't do text well)
4. **DALL-E (ChatGPT)** — convenient but produces animated/cartoonish results

## Tip
Gemini + Nano-Banana 2 + Thinking mode = best images.
