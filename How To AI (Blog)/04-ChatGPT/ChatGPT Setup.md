## Personalization Settings
Go to Settings → Personalization → Enable customization.

### Personality Presets (GPT-5.1+)
Default, Friendly, Efficient, Professional, Candid, Quirky, Cynical, Nerdy.

### Custom Instructions
GPT-5.1 follows custom instructions more tightly than ever. Set:
- How you want responses formatted
- Your role/industry context
- Tone preferences
- What to always/never do

## Model Selection
- **GPT-5 Auto** — automatically picks Instant or Thinking per prompt
- **GPT-5 Thinking** — deep reasoning (always use for important tasks)
- **GPT-5 Instant** — faster, warmer, more conversational
