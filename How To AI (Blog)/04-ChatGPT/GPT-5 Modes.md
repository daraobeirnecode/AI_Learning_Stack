## Available Modes
- **GPT-5 Auto** — picks the right sub-model automatically
- **GPT-5 Thinking** — deep reasoning (always enable for important work)
- **GPT-5 Instant** — fast, conversational

## Key Improvement: Thinking
Add "Think deeply" to the end of every prompt. Without Thinking enabled, results are significantly worse.

## GPT-5.2 Improvements
- 256K token context with 98% retrieval (vs 50% before)
- Can absorb 700+ pages of PDFs accurately
- First model to genuinely create Excel files with formulas
- Much better at math and healthcare
