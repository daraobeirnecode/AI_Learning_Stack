# Deep Research

> **Source:** Ruben Hassid, *How to AI*
> **Section:** [[How to AI - Index#📂 ChatGPT]]

---

## What It Is
ChatGPT's multi-source investigation mode. Goes beyond simple search to synthesize information from multiple sources, cross-reference claims, and produce structured reports.

## How to Use
Go to Tools → select Deep Research. Attach relevant documents. Use the 4W context prompt.

## Example Use Case
A Private Equity analyst uploads a 92-page industry benchmark PDF and asks for an investor-ready brief. Deep Research reads the full document, synthesizes findings, and produces structured analysis.
