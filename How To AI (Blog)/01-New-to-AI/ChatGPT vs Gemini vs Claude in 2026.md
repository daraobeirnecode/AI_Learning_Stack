> **Source:** Ruben Hassid, *How to AI*
> **Section:** [[How to AI - Index#📂 New to AI? Start Here]]

| Capability | ChatGPT | Claude | Gemini |
|-----------|---------|--------|--------|
| Writing | Good | **Best** | Good |
| Coding | Strong | **Best** | Strong |
| Images | DALL-E | ❌ | **Imagen 4** |
| Video | Sora | ❌ | **Veo-3** |
| File/desktop work | Agent (web) | **Cowork (local)** | Limited |

## When to Use Each
- **Claude** → writing, coding, file work, thinking
- **ChatGPT** → images, video, web agent, GPTs
- **Gemini** → images, video, free tier, Google integration
